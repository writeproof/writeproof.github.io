FAQ

Is my writing stored on your servers?
No. WriteProof runs entirely in your browser using localStorage. We never see your content.
Can someone fake a WriteProof document?
Extremely difficult. The hash chain and keystroke patterns would need to be reconstructed perfectly. The authenticity score detects suspicious patterns.
What if I paste content from my notes?
Paste events are recorded and flagged. The authenticity score adjusts accordingly. Small pastes (quotes, citations) are normal.
Can I use this for timed exams?
Yes! The timestamp data shows exactly when you started and finished writing.
How do I verify someone else's document?
Import their .json file or open their shared URL on the Verify page.
Does this work on mobile?
Yes, though the best experience is on desktop or laptop where keyboard tracking is most accurate.
