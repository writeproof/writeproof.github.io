About WriteProof

What is WriteProof?

WriteProof is a free, open-source tool that creates cryptographic proof of authentic human writing. By recording every keystroke with precise timestamps and generating tamper-proof hash chains, WriteProof lets writers demonstrate their work is genuinely theirs.

Why did we build this?

Academic integrity matters. With AI writing tools becoming ubiquitous, students and writers need ways to prove their work is authentic. But existing solutions are invasive, expensive, or unreliable.

WriteProof offers a better approach: transparent, privacy-respecting, and free. Instead of surveillance or plagiarism detection, we give writers the tools to proactively demonstrate their authorship.

Who built this?

WriteProof was created by Franklin R. Castillo, Data Science and Programming Faculty at St. Petersburg College, St. Petersburg, FL.

The project is open source and welcomes contributions from the community.
